Skills

Analysis of continuous EEG data

I have great experience working with EEG data while studying the dynamics and topography of neural components. First I use EEGLAB and its plugins such as Clean Rawdata ASR, ICLabel, and Adjust to preprocess the data then I use the mTRF toolbox to run cross-validation and find the best temporal response function to predict real data. I have learned these by EEGLAB and CNSP workshops.

Working with fMRI and ECOG data

In Neuromatch Academy summer school I had the chance to do projects with International teams and TAs, We have had team work together, to learn a little about literature and special concesrs about each kind of data, then load them in Google Colab and try to look and model them with tools such as MNE-Python and scikit-learn.
